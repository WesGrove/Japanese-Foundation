Method of Learning Individual Words:
- Use [Jisho.org](https://jisho.org/) for Definitions & Audio.
- Use [WaniKani](https://www.wanikani.com/) for Cross Reference (Purple for Vocab).
- Use [Kakimashou](https://www.kakimashou.com/) to Learn Writing.
- Practice Saying Each Word Out Loud, Individually & In Sentence.
